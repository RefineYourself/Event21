Flash Back
 
 
 
Last refine event was all about an individual's focus and team strategy to achieve a goal. 'Right at first time' approach - It is better to prevent the defects on the first sight rather than detecting it and doing followed rework.
In each team, one team member was given a spoon and rest of the team members were given two balloons. Provided balloons were in same color pair and were randomly distributed. One after the other, every team member had to pass any one of object (balloon/spoon) from their hands to the next person in a particular direction within team. Goal of the game was to make a move such that every team member will have same colored balloons in their hands. With every wrong move or passing blindly, number of rounds and time increased to reach the goal.
To learn is, it is essential to think before making a decision which is applicable even in our work so as to optimize the solution.
